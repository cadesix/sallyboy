SALLY BOY

Until I write, unconsciously often via stream of thought, I don't really know what I'm feeling, who I am mad at, or why I'm mad at all. A lot of my ability to express exactly how I feel without shame has dissipated as I've grown. I seemed to get the hang of it a bit too young for my own good, and maybe it was the people around me, or perhaps the sense of defeat within myself that forced that extremely helpful tool out of my belt at a young age. That, it seems to me, is why I yearn to be young again. To have the innocence to express how I feel, when I want to. Not to bottle it up until it comes out in anger, pushing away those closest to me. Ugh, what a waste. I think I'll figure it out one day, but in the meantime, people can enjoy my means of therapy.
